Why Choose Us

Huge School Campus

The diversification and expansion of the School through adoption of new and topically relevant courses has led to further enhancement of our infrastructure in order to fulfill our growing needs

Computer Education

Computers have taken over a major part of human activities and enabled people to have more leisure by reducing his work- load. Computer Education is started from the primary classes and is one of the optional subjects in the Senior Secondary

Music & Dance

Training of Vocal as well as instrumental music is provided according to the taste and interest of the students from Std. I to XII. Indian classical' and 'western dance' are taught in the school by experts to train, guide and help those students interested in this form of art.

Science Laboratory

The school has a well equipped laboratory of Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Computer Science, Maths and Social Studies for the students to experiment and learn because learning by doing makes concrete concept formation

Admission Procedure

Please visit us with prior appointment between 9.00 am to 3.00 pm any day from Monday to Friday and on Saturday between 9.00 am to 11.30 am.The admission office will give detailed information about the school, methodology, facilities, fee structure and guide you with the admission procedure followed by a campus tour

Lab Facilities

The facilities offered to the students include will equipped science laboratories, well stacked 18 library with books on all subjects, physics lab, chemistry lab,highly advanced computer lab, will maintained mathematics lab, splendidly furnished language lab, craft room, music room, sports room, yoga and the meditation room, a spacious multi-purpose hall, and audio visual aids.
